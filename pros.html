**Investing in real estate in Japan can be a good idea for a few key reasons:**

By Puri, on 18 Sep 2023


Investing in real estate in Japan can be a good idea for a few key reasons:


1. Asset for Family: You can leave a valuable asset for your family.
2. Social Credit: Owning a home can improve your social status.
3. Low-Interest Financing: Japan offers low-interest home loans, making buying a house more affordable.
4. Tax Deductions: You may be eligible for tax deductions related to your home loan.
5. Insurance Protection: Group credit life insurance can help if you become disabled or pass away during the loan term.
6. Larger Living Space: You can live in a bigger house with monthly payments similar to rent.
7. Facilities: Purchased properties often come with better facilities and customization options than rental properties, allowing you to create a home that suits your preferences.
8. Investment Potential: When you sell, you might get back the money paid toward your loan, potentially making a profit.
9. You can earn a good amount of money from renting out your property because there's a high demand for rental homes, especially in touristy places.
10. The value of your property can go up over time, so it may be worth more in the future than what you paid for it.


Overall, Japan can be a good place to invest in property if you do your research and get the right support.
