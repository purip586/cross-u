** various costs and taxes at different stages when buying a property **

By Puri, on 18 Sep 2023


various costs and taxes at different stages when buying a property:

1. Signing the Contract
- Pay for a Stamp Fee for the sales contract.
- Make a Deposit, usually 5% to 10% of the property's price.

2. Getting a Housing Loan:
- Pay for a Stamp Fee for the loan contract with bank. If you're getting a loan.

3. Settling the Payment:
- Pay the remaining property price.
- If you're getting a loan, consider costs like bank service fee.

4. Fire Insurance.
5. Additional Costs:
- Pay for Fixed Asset Tax.
- Pay an agent commission, usually 3% of the sales price plus 60,000JPY plus tax.

5. After Buying:
- Register your property and pay a Registry License Tax, Ownership transfer fee.

Remember that these costs and taxes can vary by location and property value. Consulting with a real estate agent is a good idea.
